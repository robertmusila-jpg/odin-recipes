ugalirecipeOdin Recipes

Ingredients

  • maize floor
  • water

Steps

  1. place the water in a sufuria and heat it to boiling
  2. add maize flour gradually as you integrate it with the boiling water using a cooking stick until it is medium thick
  3. allow it to cook under gentle heat until you feel a pleasant smell
  4. transfer the ugali onto a tray and serve hot with an accompaniment of your own choice
